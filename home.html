Welcome to our dashboard on speeding enforcement and road safety trends in Australia. This dashboard provides insights into various aspects of road safety, including fine trends, jurisdictional differences, testing activities, drug-related outcomes, and overall drug trends. Explore the different sections to gain a deeper understanding of the factors influencing road safety and enforcement outcomes across the country.

Fine Trend

Tracks speeding fines from 2020–2024, showing a rise in 2021 followed by a steady decline.

Fine Jurisdiction

Compares speeding fines across states and territories, highlighting higher enforcement in larger jurisdictions.

Testing Activity

Shows alcohol and drug testing levels by jurisdiction, with NSW leading in enforcement activity.

Drug Outcome

Breaks down positive drug test results by jurisdiction, revealing where enforcement outcomes are most common.

Drug Trend

Examines positive drug test results over time, stable until 2023 before rising in 2024.
