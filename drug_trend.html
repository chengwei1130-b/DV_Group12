Positive Drug Tests Over Time

Issue
⚠

Positive drug test outcomes are important road safety context, but the overall trend can be difficult to see from raw yearly tables.

Demonstrate Issue
⇄

The area chart shows total positive drug test outcomes from 2020 to 2024, making changes over time easier to see.

Idea and Focus
◎

Focus on the time trend of positive drug test outcomes as supporting context for the speeding enforcement story.

Analysis
⌕

Users can identify whether positive drug test outcomes increase, decrease, or peak across the selected period.

Evidence
▦

The chart uses YEAR and Positive drug tests from the final joined dataset, grouped into yearly totals.

Recommendation
☑

Use this view to discuss drug-related enforcement outcomes over time without implying individual driver-level linkage.
